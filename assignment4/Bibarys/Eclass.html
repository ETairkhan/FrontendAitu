Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mercedes-Benz E-Class?

The E-Class is a luxury sedan offering premium comfort, safety technologies, and a smooth ride from Mercedes-Benz.

Does E-Class come with 4MATIC all-wheel drive?

Yes, selected trims like the E 300 4MATIC come with advanced all-wheel drive for better control and safety.

What infotainment system is used?

The E-Class features the MBUX system with voice control, touchscreens, and smartphone integration.
